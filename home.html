PROFILE

Choi San is a South Korean singer, dancer, and performer best known as a member of the K-pop group ATEEZ, under KQ Entertainment. Born on July 10, 1999, in Namhae, South Korea, San quickly became known for his powerful stage presence and emotional expressiveness. Since ATEEZs debut in 2018, he has stood out as one of the groups most captivating performers, often praised for his charisma and ability to convey deep emotion through both dance and vocals.
Beyond his performances, San is admired for his warm personality and thoughtful nature. Fans often describe him as gentle and caring off stage, contrasting his intense, almost fierce on-stage persona. He is known for his dedication to ATEEZs fans (ATINYs), frequently expressing gratitude and motivation to keep improving. Sans duality — being both soft-spoken and incredibly passionate — has made him one of the groups most beloved members, often seen as the emotional heart of ATEEZ.

As ATEEZ continues to rise globally, Sans influence only grows stronger. His artistry shines through in every live show, music video, and fan interaction, contributing to the groups reputation as one of the most dynamic K-pop acts today. With his balance of raw talent, humility, and emotional connection, Choi San represents the new generation of idols who blend powerful performance with genuine authenticity — a combination that continues to captivate fans around the world..
